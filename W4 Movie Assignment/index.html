The Town - Home Page

The Town is a Thriller-Drama that takes place in Boston, Massachusetts where a group of criminals can't get enough of the taste of money. These four men go on multiple heists and rob multiple banks to get to where they want to be. They stop at nothing to get what they want, not police, not innocent lives, and not even the unexpected love story that unfolds throughout the movie. It seems that the group gets away with just about everything until one sunny day at Fenway Park.
